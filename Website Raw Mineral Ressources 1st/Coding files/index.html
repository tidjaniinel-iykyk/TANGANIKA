Connecting Rwandan Minerals with Global Markets

Raw Mineral Resources Company Ltd. is a leading mineral exporter based in Rwanda. With years of experience in sourcing, processing, and exporting high-quality minerals like Coltan, Cassiterite, and Wolframite, we serve as a vital link between local mining operations and the international technology and manufacturing sectors. Our commitment to reliability, compliance, and ethical sourcing is at the heart of everything we do.

Mine locations: Western Province, Rwanda

Our Core Values

  • Ethical Sourcing: We adhere to strict due diligence and traceability protocols to ensure all minerals are conflict-free.
  • Quality Assurance: Rigorous testing and quality control guarantee our products meet international standards.
  • Global Reach: We have a proven track record of exporting to major markets across Asia, Europe, and North America.
