5-Step Due Diligence Framework

A clear OECD-aligned framework we apply to suppliers and shipments.

Step 1
Establish strong management systems: policies, roles and responsibilities, and training.
Step 2
Identify & assess risks: supplier screening, source data collection and field checks.
Step 3
Design & implement a response: remediation plans and corrective actions.
Step 4
Track implementation: monitoring, audits and traceability records.
Step 5
Report & communicate: transparent reporting to partners and continuous improvement.

Focus on Safety

We prioritize the safety of our workers and communities above all else.

  • We conduct regular risk assessments to identify and mitigate potential hazards.
  • We provide comprehensive safety training and equipment to all employees.
  • We enforce strict safety protocols and regulations at all our sites.

Protecting Rights, Ensuring Fairness

We are committed to upholding human rights and promoting fair labor practices.

  • We strictly prohibit forced labour, discrimination, and harassment.
  • We provide equal opportunities for all employees, regardless of gender or background.
  • We actively engage with local communities to address their concerns and promote their well-being.

Compliance & Verification

We maintain relationships with partner organisations and keep clear documentation available.
