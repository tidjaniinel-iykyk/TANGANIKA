Our Minerals

We specialize in the sourcing and export of key conflict-free minerals essential for modern technology and industry.

Coltan (Tantalite)

A critical component in consumer electronics like smartphones and laptops. Our coltan is sourced responsibly from certified conflict-free zones.

Cassiterite (Tin Ore)

The primary source of tin, used for soldering in electronics and for creating corrosion-resistant alloys. We ensure high-grade quality and purity.

Wolframite (Tungsten Ore)

Valued for its incredible density and heat resistance, tungsten from wolframite is used in industrial machinery, aerospace, and lighting filaments.

Beryllium

Beryllium is used in aerospace and high-precision applications for its stiffness, lightweight and thermal stability. We supply beryllium in accordance with applicable safety and export regulations.
